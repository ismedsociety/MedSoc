SIH Summer Internship Program

Application Deadline: April 13th, 2026

Organization: Southern Illinois Healthcare (SIH)

Eligibility: Rising Sophomore, Junior, Senior, or graduating Senior

Details: Interns work 4-6 hours per day, Monday-Friday, in departments including Medical Technology, Nursing, Radiology, Respiratory Therapy, Physical Therapy, Occupational Therapy, and Pharmacy.

Locations: Herrin Hospital, Memorial Hospital of Carbondale, Harrisburg Medical Center, St. Joseph Memorial (Murphysboro)

Dates: June 18th - July 31st, 2026

Perks: Free lunch provided.

Young Scholars Summer STEMM Research Program

Application Deadline: March 15th, 2026

Organization: University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign

Eligibility: Rising 10th–12th graders

Details: Intensive 30–35 hours/week lab experience in neuroscience, cancer immunology, or bioengineering. Includes professional development and a final research symposium.
